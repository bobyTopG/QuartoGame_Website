Game

Quarto is a strategy board game played on a 4x4 grid with 16 unique pieces.

Each piece has 4 attributes:

size

(small, big)

fill

(solid, hollow)

shape

(round, square)

color

(light, dark)

Basic Rules

Players take turns. Your opponent selects what piece you must place. You choose where to place that piece on the board. After placing, you must select the next piece for your opponent.

Win Condition

Win by creating a row, column, or diagonal of four pieces sharing at least one attribute (all tall, all dark, all round, etc.) and announcing "Quarto!" The game ends in a draw if all pieces are placed with no winner.
